About SkinWalker or Not

SkinWalker or Not is a unique co-op multiplayer horror game where you and 1-3 other players work the night shift at a rural gas station in the 1990s. Your primary objective is to screen customers to detect skinwalkers, those perfectly disguised as humans. The game combines tense investigation and chaotic survival elements and is easy to play online for free. With simple graphics but a chilling atmosphere, Skinwalker online attracts horror fans thanks to plot twists in small details!

Game Setting

The game takes place during the night shift at a remote gas station, where customers line up to buy gas or other items. But not everyone is real; skinwalkers sneak in disguise, trying to sneak in. A single misjudgment can unleash chaos; they will escape, causing chaos. To survive until the end of your shift, you must block doors, set traps, and hide. Each night features a random layout, gradually increasing the difficulty!

How to Play SkinWalker or Not

Verify the customer's identity

Verify the ID, look up transaction records on the computer, and observe behavior (eye contact, voice, and gestures). Ask survey questions to compare information. Decide: let in or refuse. Pay attention to small details; mistakes reveal the skinwalker!

Chaos phase if the skinwalker escapes

Close doors to block exits, set traps, find hiding places, and coordinate with teammates to survive. Every second is crucial, communicate quickly and assign roles: check data, guard the gate, and defend/hide.

Controls & Gameplay Tips

  • Movement: WASD
  • Interaction: E (check, set traps)
  • Run: Shift

Tip: Move slowly to avoid guesswork, pay attention to documents, actions, and words, and play as a synchronized team; do not run around!

Comparing SkinWalker or Not with That's Not My Neighbor

Similarities: Both are horror games where you verify identities (skinwalkers/doppelgangers) through IDs, profiles, and behavior. They feature tense atmospheres, plot twists from small details, and are free to play online.

Differences: Skinwalker or Not is a multiplayer co-op game (1-3 players), set in a 1990s gas station, with a chaotic survival phase (traps, hiding places) if you're wrong. That's Not My Neighbor is a single-player game, set in a 1955 apartment building, focusing more on pure verification.
